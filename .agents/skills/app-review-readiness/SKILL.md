# App review readiness

## Required
- support email
- submission contact email
- privacy policy URL
- reviewer instructions
- dev store smoke path
- fatal-free primary routes

## Do not
- expose beta-only features to reviewer stores without explicit note
- submit with install/reinstall or pricing paths untested
